How to get to the conference

We will provide a free shuttle bus from the center of Verona (see the precise location on the map), stopping by at "Verona Porta Nuova" train station, from 7:30am to 9:30am, and back to the city center from 6pm to 8pm.


Route from "Verona Nord" Exit

Take the "Tangenziale Sud" heading towards Legnago. Take the "San Giovanni Lupatoto" exit and take the 3rd exit at the roundabout. Continue along Via Monte Pastello.


Route from "Verona Sud" Exit

Follow the "Tangenziale Sud", heading towards Legnago. Take the "San Giovanni Lupatoto" exit; at the first roundabout take the 3rd exit and take Via Ca 'Nova Zampieri. At the roundabout take the 3rd exit and continue straight along Via Monte Pastello, you will find the hotel in front of you.


Route from the Airport

You can take a bus called "Aerobus" by ATV that reaches directly the "Verona Porta Nuova" Railway Station. The departing stop is outside the "Arrivals" terminal. The service is available every day and starts every 20 minutes; it costs €6.00; last ride is at 22:50. From "Verona Porta Nuova" Station, you will find our free shuttle bus (on the day of the conference).
