How to get to the conference

We will provide a free shuttle bus from the center of Verona,stopping by at the train station, from 8am to 10am and from 6pm to 8pm


Route from Verona Nord Exit

Take the South Tangenziale in the direction of Legnago. Take the San Giovanni Lupatoto exit and take the 3rd exit to the roundabout. Continue along Monte Pastello.


Route from Verona Sud Exit

Follow the South Tangenziale in the direction of Legnago. Take the San Giovanni Lupatoto exit and at the first roundabout take the 3rd exit and take Ca 'Nova Zampieri. At the roundabout take the 3rd exit and continue straight along Monte Pastello, you will find the hotel in front of you.


Route from the Airport

There is an ATV Aerobus that arrives directly to Verona Porta Nuova Railway Station. The stop is off the "Arrivals" terminal. The service is available every day every 20 minutes and costs € 6.00; last race at 22.50. From Verona Porta Nuova Station, you will find our free shuttle bus.
